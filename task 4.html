Tribute to Mahatma Gandhi

About Mahatma Gandhi

Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi, better known as Mahatma Gandhi, was an Indian lawyer, anti-colonial nationalist, and political ethicist who employed nonviolent resistance to lead the successful campaign for India's independence from British rule.

Gandhi is known for his adherence to the principles of truth, nonviolence, and satyagraha (nonviolent resistance). His philosophy and leadership played a significant role in India's freedom struggle and inspired movements for civil rights and freedom across the world.

Famous Quotes

"Be the change that you wish to see in the world." Mahatma Gandhi
"The best way to find yourself is to lose yourself in the service of others." Mahatma Gandhi
